Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, and dental care.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact our office with your insurance details, and our friendly staff will be happy to verify your coverage.

How often should I visit the dentist?

For optimal oral health, we recommend visiting Vitals Dental for a check-up and cleaning every six months, or as advised by your dentist.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

If you experience a dental emergency, please call our office immediately. We prioritize emergency cases and will do our best to see you as soon as possible.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that dental treatments can be a significant investment. We offer various flexible payment options and financing plans to make quality dental care accessible. Please discuss with our front desk.
